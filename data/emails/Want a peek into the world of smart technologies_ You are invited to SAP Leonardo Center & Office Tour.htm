What is the SAP Leonardo Center?

SAP Leonardo Centers are the go-to places for digital inspiration and co-innovation. As the world’s largest enterprise company, SAP helps our customers digitally transform their business using IoT, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Big Data, Analytics, Data Intelligence and SAP Cloud Platform technologies. SAP Leonardo Centers are experiential showcases that bring to life digital transformation possibilities using leading-edge technologies.

 

The SAP Leonardo Center in Singapore was opened in 2018, the fifth in an expanding global network of centers. Since its inception, it has welcomed universities, start-ups, tech communities, government agencies and other organisations from around the region who want to experience future innovation first-hand.

What is the SAP STAR Program?

SAP’s Student Training & Rotation (STAR) Program is a paid rotational training program that provides a unique opportunity for students to jumpstart their careers at SAP.

 

You will:

  • Rotate across Sales, Pre-Sales and/or other tracks in different business units
  • Receive structured, on-the-job development and coaching from leaders in the technology and business fields
  • Combine full-time and part-time work across the remaining semesters of study
  • Gain hands-on, real-world experience with some of the world’s leading enterprise technologies with some of the world’s largest organisations.

Upon graduation, you will have the opportunity to secure a position within our globally-recognized SAP Academy or be considered for other opportunities within the business.
