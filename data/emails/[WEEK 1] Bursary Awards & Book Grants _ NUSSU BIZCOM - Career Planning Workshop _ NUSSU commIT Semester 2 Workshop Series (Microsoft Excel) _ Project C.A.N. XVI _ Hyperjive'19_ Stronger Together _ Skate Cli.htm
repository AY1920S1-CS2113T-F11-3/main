[NUS CSC] Project C.A.N. XVI

NUS Students' Community Service Club

NUS Students’ Community Service Club (CSC) is recruiting
Project Directors for Project C.A.N. XVI!

Project C.A.N. aims to collect canned food and dried rations from the community and redistribute these to the less privileged. 

Through its activities, Project C.A.N. hopes to encourage the spirit of giving back to the community, while cultivating compassion and creating awareness for the less privileged.

If you're interested to make a difference, do find out more and apply from: http://sp.nus-csc.org/

Feel free to also write in to nuscsc.vpsp@gmail.com with any queries you may have.
