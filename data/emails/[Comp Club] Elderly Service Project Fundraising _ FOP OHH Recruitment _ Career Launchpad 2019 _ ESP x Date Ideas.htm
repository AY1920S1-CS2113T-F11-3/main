Elderly Service Project Fundraising

Welcome back to school!

Enjoy a tasty start to the semester with delicious goodies from the Community Service Cell as we will be selling donuts from Dunkin' Donuts and milk tea from Gong Cha at $3.00 and $3.50 respectively.

Funds raised will be used to purchase basic food and cleaning items for the needy elderly, which will be given to them during our visit to them on 26th January 2019, with excess funds to be used for future elderly service projects. So come down and support us at:

  • The walkway between AS1 and the Central Library
  • The Terrace (benches near the DBS ATM)

Career Launchpad 2019

As the school term picks up, many career events will pop-up along the way, but can you make use of them? From freshmen to graduating seniors, we’ve got a line-up of talks and workshops to help you secure your dream job.

Are you curious on what different positions in Computing entail? We’re collaborating with IMDA, SoC Student Life and Corporate Relations to invite industry professionals down on 23 Jan, to share their thoughts and answer your questions about the computing industry and its various sectors.Due to popular demands, we have included specific talks on Academia, Fintech and AI talks to go in depth for you to understand more about the current trends in the industry.

If you’re looking to touch up on your job-hunting skills, we’ve partnered with SoC’s Centre for Future Graduates (CFG) and Fourth Valley to develop preparations programmes, tailored to Computing students like yourself.

Refreshments will be provided for early-birds, so what are you waiting for? Sign up today at http://bit.ly/careerlaunchpad19. Registrations close on 21 Jan 2359.
